ABOUT US

Flamingo FIBC is a leading manufacturer of high-quality FIBC bulk bags designed for industrial, agricultural, chemical and food-grade applications. We ensure premium stitching, best-grade polypropylene, and world-class production standards.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide premium FIBC solutions that combine strength, safety, and sustainability, ensuring that our clients’ products are securely transported and stored. We aim to be the trusted partner for businesses across agriculture, chemicals, food, construction, and other industries.

OUR VISION

To become a globally recognized FIBC brand known for innovation, quality, and customer-centric solutions, while contributing to the growth of Indian manufacturing on the world stage.
